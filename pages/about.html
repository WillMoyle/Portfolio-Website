UNDERGRADUATE STUDIES

In 2012, I graduated from the University of Oxford after completing a four-year undergraduate Masters degree in Mathematics. The degree was split into two parts. The first three years, for which I achieved a First, covered a wide range of topics (with a focus on Probability and Statistics). During my final Masters year, I specialised in Mathematical Physiology and Geoscience and achieved a 2.1 classification.

STARTING MY CAREER

After graduating, I joined Deloitte LLP as a Strategy Consultant. There, I gained a strong appreciation for the way in which statistical analysis (which I performed largely in Excel) can inform business decisions and shape our clientsâ€™ growth strategies. My work also had a highly qualitative side, including performing market research, interviewing senior clients and industry specialists and then distilling this information into meaningful insights, which we would then communicate to clients. This would take the form both of written documents (mostly using PowerPoint) and verbal presentations. My projects covered industries such as Media, Public Sector and Health Care and taught me how to work effectively as part of a team, including managing more junior members.

TRANSITIONING TO TECH

In 2014, I decided to pursue a more technical career, so I left Deloitte in order to undertake an MSc Computing Science degree from Imperial College, London. This course was largely based upon the C++ and Python programming languages and allowed me to explore a wide variety of technical areas, including:

  • Software Development and Object Oriented Programming (examples of some of the programs that I worked on can be found in my Portfolio)
  • Databases - covering database design, SQL and Datalog
  • Logic & Artificial Intelligence - including natural language processing using Prolog
  • Robotics - using Python to develop programs for a Lego Mindstorms and BrickPi robot (incorporating the SLAM localisation algorithm)

NOW

After achieving a high Distinction grade of 86% in my degree, I moved to New York along with my wife to begin a new chapter of my career, in which I hope to combine my technical, quantitative and qualitative skills in an exciting industry.
